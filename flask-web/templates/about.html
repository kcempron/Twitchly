Our Story

In the fall of 2018, three hackers, one physics major, and a guy from Kentucky got together to build an app with the potential to revolutionize the Twitch user experience. Twitch.tv is a video game live streaming platform that caters to gamers and esports enthusiasts of all levels. As gaming live streams have risen in popularity, the amount of content on the site has grown exponentially. As avid gamers and Twitch viewers, we often found that in order to find new streamers to follow, we would have to sift through thousands of Twitch channels in hope of finding a couple interesting channels. We wanted to create a more personalized viewer experience using the Twitch API and data analysis so that we could increase the visibility of less popular channels and finally find some cool people to watch.
